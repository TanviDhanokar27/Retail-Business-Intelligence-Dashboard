# Business Requirements

The dashboard should answer the following business requirements:

## Sales Analysis

- Calculate total revenue
- Calculate total orders
- Identify monthly sales trends
- Identify top revenue-generating categories

## Customer Analysis

- Count unique customers
- Analyze customer distribution by state

## Payment Analysis

- Identify the most preferred payment methods

## Delivery Analysis

- Calculate average delivery time
- Study the relationship between delivery time and customer reviews

## Customer Satisfaction

- Analyze review score distribution
- Identify review categories

## Dashboard

The dashboard should provide:

- KPI Cards
- Interactive visualizations
- Year filter
- Easy-to-understand business insights
