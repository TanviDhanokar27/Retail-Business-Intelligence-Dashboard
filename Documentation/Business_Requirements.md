# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name

Retail Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Platform

---

## Business Requirement

The management team of the retail company requires a centralized Business Intelligence platform to monitor business performance, analyze customer behavior, evaluate product performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

The platform should transform raw sales data into meaningful insights through analytics and interactive dashboards, enabling executives and managers to make informed strategic and operational decisions.

---

## Business Objectives

* Monitor the overall financial performance of the business.
* Analyze sales trends across different time periods.
* Identify the most profitable products and categories.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior and satisfaction.
* Evaluate regional sales performance.
* Improve inventory planning and product availability.
* Support business growth through data-driven recommendations.

---

## Business Questions

The Business Intelligence platform should answer the following questions:

1. What is the total revenue generated by the business?
2. What is the total profit earned by the business?
3. Which geographical regions generate the highest sales and profit?
4. Which products and product categories perform the best?
5. Which products generate low sales or remain unsold?
6. Are customers satisfied with the products and services?
7. Which products have the highest customer demand?
8. Which products experience delivery delays or high order volumes?
9. Who are the company's most valuable customers?
10. How are sales and profits changing over time?
11. Which months generate the highest and lowest sales?
12. What is the average order value?
13. How many customers make repeat purchases?
14. What factors negatively affect profitability?
15. What business recommendations can improve sales, customer satisfaction, and profitability?

---

## Expected Deliverables

The completed Business Intelligence platform should provide:

* Interactive Power BI dashboards
* Sales performance analysis
* Customer analytics
* Product performance analysis
* Regional sales analysis
* KPI reporting
* Business insights and recommendations
* Executive summary report

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it enables management to:

* Monitor business performance using key performance indicators (KPIs).
* Identify opportunities to increase sales and profit.
* Understand customer purchasing patterns and satisfaction.
* Make informed decisions based on data rather than assumptions.
* Improve operational efficiency through actionable insights.
