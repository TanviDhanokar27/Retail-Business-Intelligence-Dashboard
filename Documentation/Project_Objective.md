# Retail Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Platform

## Project Overview

This project aims to develop an end-to-end Business Intelligence and Sales Analytics platform for a retail business.

The platform will analyze historical retail sales data to identify trends, monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), evaluate customer purchasing behavior, assess product performance, and generate actionable business recommendations.

The final solution will include SQL analysis, Python-based data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA), Excel reports, and interactive Power BI dashboards.

---

## Business Problem

The retail company collects large volumes of sales data every day but lacks a centralized system to transform this data into meaningful business insights.

Management is unable to quickly answer important questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which regions perform the best?
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- How are sales changing over time?
- Which products are becoming less profitable?

Without these insights, decision-making becomes slow and less effective.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across products, customers, and regions.
- Measure revenue, profit, and order trends.
- Identify high-performing and low-performing products.
- Study customer purchasing behavior.
- Build interactive dashboards for decision-makers.
- Generate data-driven business recommendations.
- Forecast future sales trends (optional).

---

## Expected Outcome

The project will provide management with a centralized Business Intelligence platform that supports strategic and operational decision-making through data visualization and analytics.
