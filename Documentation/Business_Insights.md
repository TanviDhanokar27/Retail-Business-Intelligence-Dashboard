# Business Insights

## Revenue

- Revenue increased steadily throughout 2017.
- November recorded the highest revenue.

---

## Product Categories

- Health & Beauty generated the highest revenue.
- Watches & Gifts and Bed Bath Table were also strong performers.

Recommendation:

Maintain inventory levels for high-performing categories and continue promotional efforts.

---

## Customers

- São Paulo has the largest customer base.

Recommendation:

Prioritize marketing campaigns in high-performing regions while expanding into lower-performing states.

---

## Payment Methods

- Credit Card accounts for approximately 74% of all payments.

Recommendation:

Continue optimizing the credit card payment experience.

---

## Delivery Performance

- Average delivery time is approximately 13 days.

Longer delivery times are associated with lower customer review scores.

Recommendation:

Improve logistics to reduce delivery time and increase customer satisfaction.

---

## Customer Reviews

- Most customers rated their purchases as Excellent.

Recommendation:

Maintain service quality while addressing issues that lead to poor reviews.
